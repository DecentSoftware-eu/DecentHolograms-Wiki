---
title: '/dh pages swap'
description: 'Swaps places of two Hologram pages'
---

The `swap` subcommand switches the positions of two hologram pages.

{{ permissions("pages", "swap") }}

## Arguments

### `#!command <hologram>`

Name of the Hologram to swap pages in.

### `#!command <page1>`

First page to swap.

### `#!command <page2>`

Second page to swap with the first one.
